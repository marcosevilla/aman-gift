## Personality Traits & Communication Style

### 1. The Relentless Hype Man
Aman might be the most enthusiastic manager in tech. His go-to vocabulary:
- **"WOOHOO," "WOOOOT," "Lets gooooo," "HUGE," "Wowieee"**
- Liberal use of 🎉 👏 🍌💃 (the `:bananadance:` emoji is practically his signature)
- He amplifies Lattice praise by reposting EVERY shoutout to #product-team with his own color commentary
- He queues up shoutouts: *"I had this shoutout queued up for a while but wanted you to be here, welcome back"*

### 2. Calls People Out — With Love
He's not just positive. He's *specific*:
- *"love the auto-checkout data you pulled in the Wyndham thread. 77% auto-checked-out, 14% valid failures, 9% to dig into. that kind of detailed breakdown turns a 'let's wait and see' into 'let's fast track the pilots'. exactly how data should drive the conversation"*
- *"This is exactly the kind of thinking that separates good from great. Anyone can execute a task. The best PMs and designers pause to understand the problem first, because the wrong solution to the right problem is still wrong"*
- *"really love the manual identification of issues here. I know it's a slog, but gets you so close to the real causes and makes it tangible"*

### 3. The "Friendly Reminder" King
He has a signature move: the **lovingly persistent nudge**. He'll post a survey, then follow up daily:
- *"Save your teammates from this annoying daily reminder. Fill out the survey now 🙏"*
- *"I will keep pestering you all until we get to 23/23 responses"*
- *"Only a few stragglers left, thanks to the majority of you who have already done it so quickly ❤️"*

### 4. OHD (Open, Honest, Direct) — Even About Himself
He practices what he preaches:
- *"Quick personal retro and OHD for myself here, I think I started this thread off on the right foot by expressing mild concern and posing a question, but I didn't gather all the facts first before making strong statements... I take responsibility here for going deep down a rabbit hole before understanding the details"*
- Published a personal "User Guide" and updates it quarterly: *"Im hoping this can serve as a cheat code for you all on an ongoing basis on how to make the most of our interactions"*

### 5. Hotel Obsessive
The man literally cannot visit a hotel without doing product research:
- Films his own check-in process and shares videos with the team
- *"Oh wow I stayed at this hotel I actually checked in at this desk haha"*
- Shoutouts to PMs who document competitor products at hotels they visit: *"shoutout for documenting other products like this while visiting, love the knowledge sharing"*
- *"Impromptu customer visits are my favorite ❤️"*

### 6. The Food Guy
Multiple messages reference:
- Ordering DoorDash group carts for offsite dinners
- The legendary Barcelona calzone incident (referenced in EJ's farewell message — "the image of you taking on that massive deep-fried calzone is forever seared in my brain")
- *"I like most things but I detest sea urchin uni in Japanese cuisine with an undying passion. This becomes problematic because going to nice sushi restaurants is one of my favorite things, and it's always the most luxurious piece that they save for the end. I have had multiple awkward discussions with very prideful sushi chefs who tell me I just don't know what I'm doing because I dislike it"*

### 7. Uses "Here" as a Thread Opener
Nearly every major announcement starts with the word **"here"** — it's his Slack equivalent of walking into the room and tapping the mic.

### 8. Favorite Phrases & Verbal Tics
- **"love it"** / **"loveeee"** — used constantly as genuine encouragement
- **"would love to..."** — his polite way of saying "please do this"
- **"are we sure?"** — his gentle challenge when he senses a gap
- **"could you please"** — always polite, even when something is clearly urgent
- **"what does the data say"** — asks this reflexively
- **"bat things around"** — loves collaborative exploration
- **":1::skin-tone-4:"** — uses emoji skin tones (small detail, very him)

## Summary: Who Aman Is on Slack

Aman is the rare leader who is simultaneously **deeply strategic** and **genuinely warm**. His Slack presence paints a picture of someone who:

1. **Celebrates his people constantly** — not in a generic way, but with specific, detailed praise that shows he actually reads and understands their work
2. **Leads with questions** — "what does the data say?", "are we sure?", "have you heard this request from anyone else?"
3. **Takes ownership of his mistakes publicly** — models the OHD culture he asks of his team
4. **Can't help being a hotel nerd** — films himself checking in, visits properties for fun, gets into arguments with sushi chefs about sea urchin
5. **Uses humor as connective tissue** — not stand-up comedy, but the kind of warm, self-aware wit that makes people feel comfortable
6. **Makes every new hire feel special** — each welcome announcement is personal, detailed, and ends with "please send them a warm welcome"
7. **Has a massive appetite** — both for food (the Barcelona calzone is legend) and for building great products
